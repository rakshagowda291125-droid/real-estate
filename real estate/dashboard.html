Finding 01 — Location Premium

Indiranagar commands a 102% premium over Electronic City. Location is the single strongest price driver — far outweighing size or age.

Finding 02 — Size Matters

Sq.ft shows r = +0.74 correlation with price. Every 100 sq.ft increase correlates with ~₹4.2L extra in total price, independent of location.

Finding 03 — Age Discount

Properties over 10 years old are priced 18–22% lower on average. Age depreciation is steeper in high-demand zones like Koramangala.
